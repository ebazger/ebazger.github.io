Case Studies

Portfolio work focused on measurable product outcomes.

Each case highlights how I frame the problem, collaborate across teams, and apply analytics to improve customer experience and business performance.

Card Lock Experience

Card Lock Digital Experience and Transaction Analysis

Problem: Customers needed a clearer, more trustworthy card-control experience in digital channels while the business needed better visibility into behavior and usage trends.

My role: Product analytics and decision-support partner for authenticated digital journeys.

What I did: Analyzed interaction and transaction patterns, identified journey friction points, and shared structured findings with product and stakeholder teams.

Tools/skills used: Adobe Analytics, SQL, customer journey analysis, stakeholder communication.

Business/customer impact: Improved understanding of adoption and customer behavior to guide roadmap and experience decisions.

Modernization

Website and Mobile App Modernization Measurement

Problem: Large-scale modernization required consistent performance visibility and launch-readiness measurement across web and mobile platforms.

My role: Product and analytics support for a multi-year, multi-million-dollar transformation initiative.

What I did: Partnered across product, technology, and analytics teams to align measurement needs, monitor readiness, and support post-launch analysis.

Tools/skills used: Adobe Analytics, Firebase, Dynatrace, cross-functional delivery, KPI tracking.

Business/customer impact: Strengthened measurement continuity across transformation phases and improved confidence in performance interpretation.

Measurement Strategy

KPI Frameworks and Digital Analytics Strategy

Problem: New digital features often launch without a clear definition of success, limiting post-launch decision quality.

My role: Measurement strategy contributor focused on pre-launch KPI planning.

What I did: Defined KPI frameworks, tracking requirements, and reporting logic before launch; aligned teams on shared metric definitions.

Tools/skills used: KPI framework design, funnel analysis, Adobe Analytics, MarTech alignment.

Business/customer impact: Enabled faster and more consistent interpretation of performance after launch.

Decision Support

Decision Support and Stakeholder Reporting

Problem: Stakeholders needed clear, decision-ready performance views rather than disconnected data points.

My role: Analytics translator between technical data sources and business decision-makers.

What I did: Built and maintained reporting views, synthesized behavior and KPI trends, and communicated implications for product priorities.

Tools/skills used: Tableau, Power BI, Excel, storytelling with data, executive communication.

Business/customer impact: Improved alignment, prioritization, and evidence-based product decisions.

Automation

Workflow Optimization and Power Automate

Problem: Manual operational workflows created delays, inconsistent handoffs, and avoidable rework.

My role: Process improvement and automation partner.

What I did: Mapped workflow bottlenecks, redesigned process steps, and implemented Power Automate-based improvements.

Tools/skills used: Power Automate, process mapping, controls documentation, change enablement.

Business/customer impact: Reduced manual effort and improved operational reliability for teams supporting digital products.
