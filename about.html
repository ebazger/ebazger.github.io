About

I connect product thinking, data, and service-minded execution.

My work sits at the intersection of digital product management, business analysis, process improvement, and community impact.

Professional Profile

I am a technology and product management professional based in Las Vegas, Nevada. At Credit One Bank, I have worked on digital product initiatives and rotational assignments across teams including IT, Risk, Treasury, Finance, Marketing, and HR.

That cross-functional path shaped how I work: define the real problem, use data to understand tradeoffs, align stakeholders, and deliver improvements that are useful after launch.

Education and Perspective

My academic background includes graduate study at the University of Nevada, Las Vegas, with a focus on management information systems and the business side of technology.

I bring a global perspective to my work, informed by experience with U.S. financial institutions, public-sector environments, and international nonprofit initiatives.

Community and Languages

I have contributed to education-focused nonprofit work through Khatiz Organization for Rehabilitation and Hoopoe Books, including resource management, translation, and marketing support.

I speak English, Pashto, and Dari, and I care about using digital tools to expand access to knowledge, learning, and opportunity.
