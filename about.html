About

I connect digital product strategy, analytics, and service-minded execution.

My work sits at the intersection of banking technology, customer experience, business analysis, digital measurement, and data-driven product decisions.

Senior Digital Product Management & Business Analytics

In my current role, I focus on the intersection of digital product strategy, business analytics, customer experience, and data-driven decision-making within the banking sector.

I manage and support the bank's existing website and mobile app from a digital product and analytics perspective, helping ensure both platforms are maintained effectively, perform reliably, and continue to meet business and customer needs.

A major part of my work involves analyzing digital performance, translating customer behavior and platform data into meaningful insights, and sharing accurate reporting with stakeholders to support informed strategic decisions.

Digital Modernization and Measurement

I support the bank's multi-year, multi-million-dollar modernization of its mobile app and website, a transformation focused on improving the digital ecosystem, customer experience, and measurement capabilities across digital channels.

My responsibilities include defining KPI measurement strategies before launch, documenting tracking requirements, aligning analytics frameworks with business goals, and helping ensure new digital journeys can be measured accurately.

I use Adobe Analytics, MarTech platforms, and other digital analytics tools to support tracking, reporting, performance measurement, and optimization before and after launch.

Professional Profile

I am a technology and product management professional based in Las Vegas, Nevada. At Credit One Bank, I have worked on digital product initiatives and rotational assignments across teams including IT, Risk, Treasury, Finance, Marketing, and HR.

That cross-functional path shaped how I work: define the real problem, use data to understand tradeoffs, align stakeholders, and deliver improvements that are useful after launch.

Education and Perspective

My academic background includes graduate study at the University of Nevada, Las Vegas, with a focus on management information systems and the business side of technology.

I bring a global perspective to my work, informed by experience with U.S. financial institutions, public-sector environments, and international nonprofit initiatives.

Community and Languages
