About

Digital Product Manager with a measurement-first mindset.

I help digital banking teams define success before launch, measure performance after launch, and translate customer behavior into clearer product decisions.

What differentiates my approach

I do not only support feature delivery. I help teams define how success will be measured before launch and how to interpret outcomes after launch so roadmaps can be shaped by evidence instead of assumptions.

My work combines product strategy, customer journey thinking, KPI frameworks, and stakeholder communication across web and mobile banking experiences.

Core strengths

I operate across product, analytics, and business teams to connect priorities, constraints, and outcomes. This includes defining measurement plans, synthesizing digital behavior insights, and supporting executive-ready reporting.

Tools and methods include Adobe Analytics, MarTech platforms, BI reporting, funnel analysis, adoption tracking, and post-launch performance monitoring.

Professional perspective

Based in Las Vegas, I bring digital banking experience, cross-functional delivery discipline, and a practical approach to continuous improvement. I focus on initiatives that make customer experiences simpler and business decisions smarter.

I also bring a global, service-oriented perspective informed by community and education-focused work.
